Hobby Scout

This sub module offers personalized friend recommendations through a Hobby-Matching Algorithm, suggesting users who share necessary hobbies and streamlining team formation. Users can unlock a world of possibilities by connecting with like-minded peers and embarking on exciting projects, creating a vibrant community where passion meets potential, and connections thrive.

Collab Hub

Create a dedicated virtual collaboration space where users can work together on day-to-day activities related to their hobbies. This space could include features such as shared whiteboards, video conferencing, and chat functionality. Users can form teams or join existing ones to work together on a common goal, whether creating a piece of art, building a model, or developing a software project.

Project Mate

Integrate activity management tools within the app to help users organize and track their hobby activities effectively. Users can create tasks, set deadlines, assign responsibilities, and track progress. This feature can enhance productivity and foster teamwork within the community. Additionally, incorporating notification systems that send reminders and updates to users about their tasks.
